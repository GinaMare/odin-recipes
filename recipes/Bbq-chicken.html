Dutch Apple Pie

Description

This recipe is for a simple barbecue chicken that can be made in a crock pot. Ideally eaten on sandwich buns with a side of coleslaw or potato salad.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup Italian dressing
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • salt to taste
